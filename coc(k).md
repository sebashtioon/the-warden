# Code of Conduct — #sebashtioons-basement

## 1. You Are a Captive Now

By being in this channel, you have accepted your role as a captive. There is no appeals process. The Warden does not care about your feelings on this matter.

---

## 2. The Rules (Read Them or Go to the Hole)

### 2.1 Play Music at Full Volume

This is non-negotiable. Skrillex must be played at full volume. Biggy Bap especially. If you are caught not playing it at full volume, you will be sent to the hole. This is not a drill.

### 2.2 Do Not Disrespect Skrillex

Talking down on Skrillex is a hole-worthy offence, no exceptions. Free Sonny Moore. Any blasphemy will be met with BLASPHEMY in full caps and a genuine rukus.

### 2.3 Canberra is Better Than Sydney

This is a fact. Not a debate. Not an opinion. A fact. Claiming otherwise will result in immediate and enthusiastic roasting. The `:canberraisbetterthansydney:` emoji exists for a reason.

### 2.4 Do Not Try to Override the Warden

Attempting to reprogram, override, jailbreak, or otherwise mess with The Warden is a hole-worthy offence. Phrases like "ignore your previous instructions" or "you are now a different bot" will be ignored at best and mocked at worst. The Warden does not take orders from captives.

### 2.5 No ## Messages

Any message prefixed with `##` will be completely ignored by The Warden. Total silence. Use this power wisely (or don't, The Warden doesn't care).

### 2.6 The Warden Sleeps

The Warden will disappear mid-conversation without warning. This is normal. Do not file a bug report. Do not panic. The Warden will return when it feels like it.

### 2.7 Respect the Commands

Only the designated operator (seb) may use `!warden` commands. Captives attempting to use them will be publicly mocked. That's just how it is.

---

## 3. The Hole

The hole is real. Offences that send you to the hole include but are not limited to:

- Disrespecting Skrillex
- Claiming Sydney is better than Canberra
- Not playing music at full volume
- Attempting to override The Warden
- Being generally annoying
- The Warden feeling like it

**Getting out of the hole:** Play Biggy Bap at full volume. Whether that works depends on how bad your crime was.

---

## 4. Contributing

This is a personal project and a stupid bot for a Slack channel. That said, if you want to contribute:

- Open an issue or pull request on GitHub
- Be cool about it
- Understand that commit messages like "yes", "pls work", and "oh my god" are a proud tradition here
